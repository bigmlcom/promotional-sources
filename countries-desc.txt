A dataset created by merging various economic and sociologic indicators for countries globally. The features are: 
Employment Rate,Alcohol Consumption per adult (lt),Expend/student Primary,Expend/student Secondary,Expend/student Tertiary,CO2 Emissions per person (Mt Tons),Gender Equality Ratio,GDP per capita

**Source**

The gender equality ration is from the [Global Gender Gap Report](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_Gender_Gap_Report)

The remaining indicators were provided through [Gapminder](http://www.gapminder.org/data/)

* Aged 15+ employment rate (%): [International Labour Organization](http://www.ilo.org/global/lang--en/index.htm) 
* Alcohol consumption per adult (15+), litres: [World Health Organization](http://www.who.int/) 
* Expenditure per student, primary (% of GDP per capita): [World Bank](http://www.worldbank.org/) 
* Expenditure per student, secondary (% of GDP per capita): [World Bank](http://www.worldbank.org/) 
* Expenditure per student, tertiary (% of GDP per capita): [World Bank](http://www.worldbank.org/) 
* CO2 emissions (tonnes per person): [CDIAC (Carbon Dioxide Information Analysis Center)](http://cdiac.ornl.gov/)
* GDP/capita (US$, inflation-adjusted): [World Bank](http://www.worldbank.org/)
