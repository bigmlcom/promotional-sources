This dataset relates demographic information for counties in the U.S. with the outcome of the 2012 Presidential Election for that county.

**Source**

Demographics from [Esri](http://www.esri.com/) at [Azure Datamarket](https://datamarket.azure.com/dataset/esri/keyusdemographicstrial)

2012 Elections results from [The Guardian Datablog](http://www.guardian.co.uk/news/datablog/2012/nov/07/us-2012-election-county-results-download)

2012 Elections results source: [US presidential election results by county](https://www.google.com/fusiontables/DataSource?docid=1qcQLqrAIAe3RcEfdWSm_QcXMLmteVg4uSpSs1rM)
